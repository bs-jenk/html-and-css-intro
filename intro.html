Princess Donut

Character Profile

Description

Princess Donut is a tortoiseshell Persian cat who can talk. Although she couldn't talk originally, she gained sentient intelligence after eating an enhanced pet biscuit. She has a very distinctive personality, identifies as a princess, and loves to get her own way.

Details:

  • Name: Princess Donut
  • Nickname: Donut
  • Full Name & Titles: Grand Champion, Breed Winner, National Winner Princess Donut the Queen Anne Chonk
  • Species: Felis catus (cat)
  • Coat colour: Tortoiseshell (example here)
  • Characteristics:
    1. Vain
    2. Likes pretty things
    3. Afraid of getting dirty
    4. Extremely protective and possessive
    5. Pretends to be less intelligent than she is
